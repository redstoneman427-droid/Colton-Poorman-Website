Mine & Yours (feat. Liam Cuff)

Release date: February 14, 2025

Type: Single

Mine & Yours is the second single from Long Time Coming. Similar to Make It Back, it's about being in love with someone who you aren't quite sure loves you back, but this song is more about trying to win over that person with your own love, rather than deny it.
