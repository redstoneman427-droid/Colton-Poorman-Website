Imperfect is Limiter's debut album. We wrote and recorded it throughout 2022-2023, and we took a lot of inspiration from Foo Fighters, AC/DC, and a lot of other rock artists between those eras. Almost all of the lyricism was done by Kellen, so I can't really comment on any lyrical themes or meanings.
