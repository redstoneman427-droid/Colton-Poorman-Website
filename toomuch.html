VSMS0012

Too Much

Release date: July 18, 2025

Too Much is the third single from Long Time Coming. It sounds like it's about forcing a failed relationship to work because you want it to just so bad, and it's kind-of about that, but its really about me wrestling with my own ambitions as a musician. My relationship with music is a lot like a toxic, codependent relationship sometimes, and this song is about that.
